Research & Projects

Lung Damage Detection System
Designed and trained a convolutional neural network for multi-class classification of lung diseases, including pneumonia and lung cancer. Integrated the model with a real-time ML pipeline using TensorFlow Extended (TFX), ensuring smooth data ingestion, automated preprocessing, and scalable training capabilities in production-like environments. The system serves as a diagnostic support tool with potential to reduce manual diagnosis time by 30% and improve early detection accuracy in clinical settings.
Technologies: TensorFlow, TensorFlow Extended (TFX), CNN, Seaborn
Multiclass Classification Using Neural Networks
Developed and optimized a neural network on 50,000+ multi-class images, achieving 92% test accuracy through extensive hyperparameter tuning and architecture experimentation. Validated performance with confusion matrices and learning curves, demonstrating a 15% improvement over baseline models while ensuring robustness and interpretability. Compiled comprehensive results including visualizations, error analyses, and comparisons against alternative approaches.
Technologies: TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Google Colab
Customer Feedback Classification System
Developed and deployed a Naive Bayes classifier to categorize 10,000+ customer feedback samples, achieving 91% accuracy through systematic preprocessing including tokenization, stopword removal, and vectorization. Enhanced model performance by 18% through advanced feature engineering and iterative hyperparameter tuning. Conducted thorough evaluation using precision (89%), recall (87%), and F1-score (88%) metrics.
Technologies: Python, Scikit-learn, NLP, Feature Engineering
California Housing Price Prediction
Built and validated regression models to predict California housing prices with strong predictive accuracy. Conducted in-depth exploratory data analysis, uncovering correlations between housing prices and 10+ demographic and economic indicators including income levels, population density, and proximity to the ocean. Compared multiple regression approaches (linear, ridge, lasso) to select the best-performing model for deployment.
Technologies: Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Regression Analysis
